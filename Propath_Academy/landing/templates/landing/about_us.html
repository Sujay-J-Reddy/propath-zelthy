About Us

We exist to make difference in thinking capabilities of students in a sustainable manner for creative confident tomorrow, we offer our course fostering cognitive development ability to think & reason along with learning capabilities which grows the community forward in the child academics.

Our Mission

At Propath Academy, our mission is to enhance the thinking capabilities of students in a sustainable manner, fostering a creative and confident tomorrow.


Our courses are designed to promote cognitive development, reasoning skills, and learning abilities, advancing the academic growth of children and benefiting the broader community.


Propath Academy stands out by engaging students in active brain fitness through the enrichment of visual learning capabilities in a disciplined environment.


Our growth is driven by a commitment to women empowerment and building entrepreneurship within our business. We strive for the satisfaction of our franchisees, course instructors, and clients by positively shaping the future of students.
