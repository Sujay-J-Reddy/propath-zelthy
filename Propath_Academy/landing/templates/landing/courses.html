Courses

Abacus

An abacus is an ancient tool used for performing mathematical calculations. The word "abacus" is derived from the Latin word "abax," which means a flat surface or table. This instrument consists of beads that can be moved to execute various arithmetic operations, including addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, as well as calculations involving decimals and square roots.

Vedic Maths

Vedic Maths is a mathematical system that enhances speed and accuracy in calculations through various shortcut tricks and techniques, known as Vedic Sutras. Originating in India, the concept of Vedic Maths was developed by Shri Bharti Krishna Tirthaji, who is regarded as the father of Vedic Maths. He compiled all the Vedic Sutras in his book, which serve as the foundation for rapid calculation methods. These techniques can be applied to perform quick and efficient calculations in addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, decimals, square roots, cube roots, HCF, LCM, and many other advanced mathematical operations. Children can learn and benefit from these methods to improve their mathematical skills.

Handwriting

A Handwriting Course is designed to improve one's handwriting skills, focusing on enhancing legibility, speed, and overall presentation. This course employs various techniques and exercises to develop better hand coordination, consistency, and style in writing. Participants will learn proper letter formation, spacing, and alignment, as well as tips for maintaining a comfortable and efficient writing posture. Suitable for all age groups, a handwriting course can greatly benefit students, professionals, and anyone looking to refine their handwriting for personal or professional purposes.
